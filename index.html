Frequently Asked Questions

What's your typical project timeline?

Most projects are completed within 6-8 weeks, following our proven 4-phase process: Discovery (1 week), Design (2 weeks), Development (3-4 weeks), and Launch (1 week). Complex projects may require additional time.

What ongoing support do you provide?

We offer comprehensive maintenance packages including 24/7 monitoring, security updates, performance optimization, and content updates. Our support team is available via phone, email, and chat during business hours.

How do you handle website security?

Security is paramount. We implement SSL certificates, regular security audits, automated backups, and advanced firewall protection. All our sites include malware scanning and DDoS protection.

Can I update the website myself?

Yes! We build all sites with user-friendly content management systems. We provide training and documentation to ensure your team can easily manage content updates independently.

What's included in the initial consultation?

Our free consultation includes a comprehensive needs analysis, competitor research, preliminary design concepts, and a detailed proposal with timeline and pricing options.
